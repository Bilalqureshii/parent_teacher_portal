Welcome to Student Tracker Portal

Our Parent-Teacher Portal is designed to facilitate seamless communication and collaboration between parents and teachers. Whether you're a parent looking to stay informed about your child's academic progress or a teacher seeking to engage parents in their child's education, our portal has you covered!

With our platform, you can:

  • Track your child's academic performance and progress in various subjects.
  • Stay updated on important school events, assignments, and announcements.
  • Communicate directly with your child's teachers to discuss their development, address concerns, and collaborate on strategies for improvement.
  • Access resources and educational materials to support your child's learning journey.
  • Participate in parent-teacher conferences and meetings to foster a collaborative approach to education.

If you're new to the portal, sign up now to create your account and begin exploring the various features and resources available. If you're already a member, simply sign in to access your account and stay connected with your child's educational journey!
